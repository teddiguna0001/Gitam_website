About GITAM Deemed University

GITAM (Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management) is a private deemed university established in 1980 in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. With a rich legacy of over four decades, GITAM has evolved into a multidisciplinary institution offering world-class education across engineering, management, sciences, humanities, law, pharmacy, and more.

🏛️ Our Vision

To become a global leader in higher education by fostering innovation, research, and holistic development, empowering students to contribute meaningfully to society and the nation.

🎯 Our Mission

  • To provide transformative education that blends academic excellence with ethical values.
  • To promote cutting-edge research and innovation across disciplines.
  • To nurture global citizens with leadership qualities, social responsibility, and entrepreneurial spirit.
  • To build strong industry-academia partnerships for enhanced learning and career opportunities.

🌟 Why GITAM?

  • NAAC A+ Accreditation — Recognized for academic excellence and quality education.
  • Global Collaborations — Partnerships with leading universities in the USA, UK, Australia, and Europe.
  • State-of-the-Art Campus — Sprawling campuses in Visakhapatnam, Hyderabad, and Bengaluru with modern infrastructure.
  • Industry-Ready Curriculum — Programs designed with input from industry experts to meet current market demands.
  • Research & Innovation — Dedicated research centers, incubation cells, and startup support for students.
  • Diverse Student Community — Students from over 25 states and 15+ countries, creating a vibrant multicultural environment.

📚 Academic Programs

GITAM offers over 100+ programs across multiple disciplines:

  • Engineering & Technology — BTech, MTech, PhD in various specializations
  • Management — BBA, MBA, PhD in Management
  • Sciences — BSc, MSc, PhD in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Life Sciences
  • Humanities & Social Sciences — BA, MA, and PhD programs
  • Law — BA LLB, BBA LLB, LLM, and PhD in Law
  • Pharmacy — BPharm, MPharm, and PhD programs
  • Architecture & Planning — BArch and MPlan programs
