🎓 Undergraduate Courses at GITAM

GITAM (Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management), now known as GITAM (Deemed to be University), is a multi-campus university with locations in Visakhapatnam, Hyderabad, and Bengaluru. Founded in 1980, it offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines, including technology, management, pharmacy, architecture, and more.

GITAM emphasizes a multidisciplinary approach to education, fostering holistic development and preparing students for careers through industry collaborations and research opportunities.

🌟 Key Aspects of GITAM

  • Multidisciplinary Approach: Wide array of academic programs across different disciplines, promoting both specialization and interdisciplinary learning.
  • Campus Locations: Campuses in Visakhapatnam, Hyderabad, and Bengaluru, each offering a unique learning environment.
  • Industry Connections: Active engagement with industries through partnerships, advisory boards, and placement initiatives.
  • Research Focus: Encourages research and innovation through TEPE and Makerspace facilities.
  • Holistic Development: Aims to develop well-rounded individuals with strong ethical values and leadership skills.
  • Placement Success: Strong placement record with various companies actively recruiting from its campuses.
  • Global Exposure: Opportunities for students to gain global perspectives through international collaborations and exchange programs.
  • Infrastructure: Modern infrastructure including well-equipped laboratories, libraries, and other facilities.
  • Vision and Values: Guided by a vision to be a premier knowledge-driven institution grounded in integrity and compassion.
