📊 Fee Structure

GITAM Deemed University offers a transparent and affordable fee structure across all programs. Below is the detailed fee breakdown for various UG and PG courses for the academic year 2025-26.

  • Semester-wise Payment
  • EMI Options Available

📝 Additional Fees
