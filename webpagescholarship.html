🎓 Scholarships at GITAM

GITAM University (Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management) offers various scholarships based on merit, entrance exam performance, and financial need. These scholarships can cover a significant portion of tuition fees, potentially up to 100%. Additionally, some scholarships include hostel fee support.

📋 Types of Scholarships

🏆 Merit-Based

Merit Scholarships

Awarded based on performance in entrance exams like:

  • GAT (GITAM Admission Test)
  • GBAT (GITAM Business Admission Test)
  • JEE Main
  • EAMCET
  • CUET
  • SAT
  • CAT / MAT / NMAT / CMAT
💵 Need-Based

Need-Based Scholarships

Additional financial support for students from lower-income families. These scholarships help make quality education accessible to all deserving candidates.

🏠 Hostel

Hostel Scholarships

Discounts on hostel fees, particularly for students who have received tuition fee waivers. This helps reduce the overall cost of education.

⭐ Special

Special Scholarships

Dedicated scholarships for specific groups including:

  • CRPF personnel families
  • Distinguished individuals
  • Sports achievers
  • Economically weaker sections

📊 Scholarship Details

💰 Scholarship Slabs

GITAM offers scholarship slabs of:

  • 100% — Full tuition fee waiver
  • 75% — Three-fourths tuition fee waiver
  • 60% — Three-fifths tuition fee waiver
  • 40% — Two-fifths tuition fee waiver
  • 25% — One-fourth tuition fee waiver
  • 15% — Partial tuition fee waiver

📝 Entrance Exam Scholarships

  • Scholarships based on entrance exam scores (like GAT) are applicable for the first year.
  • Continuation in subsequent years is contingent on maintaining a CGPA of 8.0 or above.

🎯 Post-Interview Adjustment

  • Students eligible for 60% or higher scholarships through merit may undergo a personal interview.
  • Scholarship slab may be adjusted based on interview performance.

⏳ First-Come, First-Served

  • Scholarship seats are limited and offered on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • Early application is highly recommended to secure your scholarship.

📚 Additional Information

🧮 Scholarship Calculator

GITAM provides a Scholarship Calculator to help students estimate their potential scholarship amount based on their entrance exam scores. Visit the admissions portal to use this tool.

🏦 Education Loans

GITAM also supports students with education loans through partnerships with leading banks. Assistance is provided for loan processing and documentation.

🏛️ Government Scholarships

Students may also be eligible for various government scholarships offered by the central and state governments.

📝 How to Apply

  1. Entrance Exam-Based: Apply for the scholarship through the respective entrance exam application portals.
  2. Need-Based: Apply through GITAM's scholarship application portal, often linked to the admissions portal.
  3. Special Scholarships: Follow the application procedures specific to the particular scholarship.
